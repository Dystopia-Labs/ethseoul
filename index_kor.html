ETH SEOUL

March 29-31, 2024 Neopin Building, Seoul, Korea (See Map Here)

ETH Seoul is a 3 day hackathon that takes place from March 29-31 in South Korea. ETH Seoul started in 2022 and this year will be our 3rd hackathon. We urge you to come out, meet our vibrant builder community, and BUIDL the latest cutting edge tech in the Ethereum ecosystem!
